## Key Takeaways

### What Worked Well

[3-5 success factors]

**Example:**
1. **Phased Rollout:** Starting with a pilot (3 repos) allowed for optimization before full deployment
2. **Executive Sponsorship:** VP Engineering championed the initiative, ensuring adoption
3. **Training Investment:** Dedicated onboarding sessions got teams productive fast
4. **Custom Configuration:** Tailoring audit rules to TechCorp's standards maximized value
5. **Regular Check-ins:** Weekly meetings with Evolving-sun CSM caught issues early

### Lessons Learned

[Advice for other customers]

**Example:**
- **Start Small:** Pilot with friendly teams first, learn, then scale
- **Set Clear Metrics:** Define success criteria upfront (we tracked error rate, review time, quality score)
- **Involve Security Early:** Getting CISO buy-in prevented compliance blockers
- **Plan for Change Management:** Communicated benefits clearly to overcome resistance
- **Leverage Support:** Don't try to figure everything out alone - lean on the vendor
